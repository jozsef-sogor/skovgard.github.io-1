Zucchini Sushi

You can’t make a mistake with this recipie. Making it is a piece of cake but the taste is way better and also it is healthier. Don’t be intimidated by the name of it it is easier to make then it sounds.

Making Your Zucchini Sushi

  1. Using a vegetable peeler, slice each zucchini into thin flat strips
  2. Place zucchini on paper towel-lined plate to sit while you prep the rest of your ingredients.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine cream cheese, Sriracha, and lime juice.
  4. On a cutting board, lay zucchini slices down horizontally (so that the long side is facing you). Spread a thin layer of cream cheese on top, then top the left side with a pinch each of crab, carrots, avocado, and cucumbers.
  5. Starting from the left side, tightly roll each zucchini slice. Sprinkle with sesame seeds before serving.
