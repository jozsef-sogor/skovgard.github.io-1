Meals

Having a nutritious and well-balanced meal is essential to perform well in school. A tasty meal is an easy way to impress anyone. The following recipes will help you get your body and mind on track or to have a dinner for a date. The results will speak for themselves.

Recipies

Hummus Chicken Tortilla Wraps

You can plate it and eat at home or just pack them and take them for lunch at school. You can play with the filling and ingredients to match exactly your taste and needs. This is a really easy recipie and doesn’t take much time to make.

Braised Chicken Breast in Milk

This meal is one of my favorite meals and I can assure you that it’ll become one of yours too. The flavours work so well together that you will want to cook it every other day. On top the sensational taste it is actually healthy due to the chicken breast and the greens.
